YOU CAN HELP SAVE OUR PLANET.


01. GOVERNMENTAL OUTREACH

  • Write a letter or make a phone call to a local leader, representative, or senator explaining your concern about climate change and encouraging them to act

  • Vote for leaders who believe in climate change and support climate action

  • Sign a petition calling for climate action

  • Attend an event, such as a march or rally, to raise awareness about environmentalism and climate action

02. INVOLVEMENT/VOLUNTEERING

  • Spread awareness on social media or to those you know (for example, share this site!)

  • Join or donate to a nonprofit that fights for environmentalism or wildlife protection

  • Volunteer locally in cleanup initiatives or tree-planting events

  • Join a chapter of an organization like the Sierra Club, the Sunrise Movement, or Citizens Climate Lobby
